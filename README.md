## üìà Visualizations & Insights
1. Stay Trends ("How many stays per day?")
 
 
     Type: Area Chart.

     Insight: Tracks daily volume, highlighting a significant peak in early October, suggesting a specific event or seasonal demand surge.

2. Temporal Analysis ("Weekdays or Weekends?")


     Type: Bar Chart.

     Insight: Compares room nights across the week. High performance on Tuesday-Friday indicates a strong Business/Corporate traveler base, while Sunday shows th lowest occupancy.

4. Guest Segmentation ("Who stays with us?")


      Type: Horizontal Bar Chart.
      
      Insight: Breaks down stays by loyalty tier. Non-members represent the largest group (1.9K), followed by Essential members (1.0K), indicating a significant opportunity for loyalty program conversion.

4. Booking Lead Time ("How far away they book?")
 
 
     Type: Histogram/Column Chart.


    Insight: A massive majority of guests book within a week of their stay. This suggests a "Last-Minute" booking culture, requiring flexible pricing strategies.
